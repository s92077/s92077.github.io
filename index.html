About Me

I am a mathematician working in dynamical systems, ergodic theory, and fractal geometry. I recently completed my PhD at the University of Oulu under the supervision of Professor Meng Wu, Professor Ville Suomala, and Associate Professor Sascha Troscheit. My research investigates the geometric and stochastic properties of chaotic systems — in particular, the dimensional theory of fractal measures and sets arising from dynamics.
